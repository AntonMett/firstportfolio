About me

Why programming?

When I wrote my first script, I realized that I have missed so much in my life! For the past 6 years I have been working as a taxi driver in Estonia's biggest taxi company "Tulika Takso''. Last year I decided to move on, so I became a bus driver in "Tallinna Linnatranspordi AS". It felt like I was going in the wrong direction in my life and took a step back and analyzed this situation. I realized that I love learning, challenges and the idea of "ENGINE BUILDING". I googled it and here I am. After my first script I wrote in Python I can't live a day without coding.

Sports and hobbies

I used to play football and cycling. Right now my family and learning drain all my energy so I don't have spare energy or time to invest in beloved sports, but I believe that someday I will have time for those. My hobbies have changed over time, but they all stay near the idea of engine building - building scooters from zero, car tuning, playing games with the same concept. My main passion is building PC's, yes it gets boring after 2-3 months but I keep coming back to it after 2-3 years, when technologies have changed and I have to learn new technologies and make "new" engines.
